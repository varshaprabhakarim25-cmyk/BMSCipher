Academics

Programs designed for strong foundations and modern skills.

Programs

Undergraduate Programs

Comprehensive B.E streams in CSE, ECE, MECH, CIVIL, ISE, and EEE with industry-aligned electives and project-based learning.

Postgraduate Programs

M.Tech and specialized postgraduate pathways offering advanced domain expertise, technical depth, and applied research opportunities.

PhD Programs

Doctoral research with faculty mentorship, lab facilities, publication support, and collaboration opportunities across interdisciplinary domains.

Academic Facilities and Curriculum

Academic Facilities

Smart classrooms, centralized library, digital resources, seminar halls, mentoring cells, and innovation spaces ensure an engaging learning environment.

Curriculum

Outcome-based curriculum with foundational courses, electives, capstone projects, internships, communication training, and professional ethics.

Laboratories

Computing Labs

Modern systems for coding, AI, cloud simulation, cybersecurity practice, and software architecture exercises.

Core Engineering Labs

Department-specific practical facilities for electronics design, mechanical systems, civil testing, and electrical simulation.

Innovation Labs

Prototype-driven spaces for interdisciplinary projects, hackathons, and startup exploration under faculty and industry guidance.
